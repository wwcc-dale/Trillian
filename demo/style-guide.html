    Automatic fullscreen viewer applied to every <img> not wrapped in a link. Click to open; Escape or click backdrop to close. Alt text becomes the caption. No markup required.

    Pace Dashboard

    components/pace-dashboard.js

    Reading rate gauge. Measures actual pages/day ÷ required pages/day — pace = 1.0 means exactly on schedule. Reads engagement data written by page-tracker.js. Optionally projects program milestone dates based on credit accumulation rate.

    - pace-dashboard
- total-pages:  48
- start-date:   2026-01-05
- end-date:     2026-05-01
- target-date:  2026-04-15   ← optional on/off-track pill
- pace-metric:  counted       ← visited | counted | completed
- program-start-date: 2025-09-01
- credits-earned:     15
- credits-per-course: 5
    KeyDefaultDescription
    total-pages—Number of Canvas pages in the course
    start-date—Course start date (YYYY-MM-DD)
    end-date—Course end date (YYYY-MM-DD)
    target-date—Optional deadline for on/off-track pill
    pace-metriccountedvisited | counted | completed
    program-start-date—When student entered the program (enables outlook section)
    credits-earned0Credits fully completed before this course
    credits-per-course5Credit value of the current course
    milestone-mini20Credits for short certificate
    milestone-cert45Credits for certificate
    milestone-degree90Credits for AAS degree
